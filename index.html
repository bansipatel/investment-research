Bansi Patel, CFA

Investment Research

Bansi Patel, CFA is a credit and equity investor with institutional experience across public credit, fixed income portfolio management, high yield research, and strategic advisory. He has held investment roles at MetLife Investment Management, one of the largest U.S. insurance asset managers, and Polen Capital Credit, and has advised private equity clients on value creation at Strategy&. He holds an MBA from NYU Stern School of Business, where he served as a portfolio manager for the Michael Price Student Investment Fund under Professor Aswath Damodaran, and is a CFA charterholder. His analytical framework centers on downside protection, cash flow durability, capital structure, and leverage — applied consistently across credit and equity. The research on this site represents independent investment analysis.
